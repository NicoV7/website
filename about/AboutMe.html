After building my first PC in highschool, I fell into the rabbit hole of Skyrim modding. Building large mod playlists to push the game to its limits became a hobby of mine, as well as making some light mods myself. I started in 2016 and, 6 years and 20+ iterations later, I had a functional playlist of over 2000 mods taking over 400GBs. 2022 was the last year I made custom builds as newer tools came out such as Wabbajack, which allows for a community of debuggers.
